Developed RL agents for Street Fighter II using **PPO** 
and **LSTM-PPO** via OpenAI Gym Retro.

**Key Results:**
- Achieved **96% win rate** against built-in AI
- Improved training efficiency by 30% via grayscale 
  encoding and frame skipping
- Designed domain-inspired reward functions for 
  adaptive agent behavior

**Awarded Best Final Year Project** (top 1 of department)
at University of Macau.

**Tools:** PyTorch, Gym Retro, Stable-Baselines3
